How it works Close

This is a basketball shooting practice tracker app based on Stephen Curry routine. The process is very simple:

1. Try 5 shoots in every position marked in the court

2. Select the amount of successful shoots in each position

3. Once you have entered every number, you will see the submit button appears in the basket

4. When you click, your success rate will be displayed at the top of the screen.

5. The result will be automatically saved into your local device memory, you can follow your progress through the History and Stats pages

Now you're ready to take your shooting practice to the next level!!
