How it works Close

This is an basketball shooting practice traker app based on Stephen Curry routine. The proccess is very simple:

1. Try 5 shoots in every position marked in the court

2. Select the amount of successfull shoots in each possition

3. Once you have entered every number, you will see the submit button appears in the basket

4. Once you click on it you will see the success rates on the top of the screen

5. The result will be automatically saved into your local device memory, you can follow your progress throught the history and stats pages

Now you're ready to take your shooting practice to the next leve!!
